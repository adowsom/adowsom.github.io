Heart-shaped traffic lights spotted in Changsha on Chinese Valentine's Day

27 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

Heart-shaped traffic lights were spotted in a southern Chinese city on Chinese Valentine’s Day (August 25).
The video, filmed in the city of Changsha in Hunan Province, shows the red traffic lights forming a heart shape.
According to reports, the police officers changed the shape of the red traffic lights to heart on Chinese Valentine’s Day to encourage people to obey the traffic rules.
The video was provided by local media with permission.
