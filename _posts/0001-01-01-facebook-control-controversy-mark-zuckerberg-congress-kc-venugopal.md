Facebook is caught in a political controversy in India. According to the American newspaper Wall Street Journal, Facebook has helped the RSS and the ruling BJP, considered ideologically close to the Sangh. Now the opposition is aggressive about this issue. The Congress has once again demanded Facebook to investigate the entire matter.
