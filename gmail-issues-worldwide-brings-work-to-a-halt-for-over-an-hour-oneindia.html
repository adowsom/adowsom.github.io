Gmail issues worldwide brings work to a halt for over an hour Oneindia News

26 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

On Thursday morning, Gmail suffered a massive outage worldwide with users unable to upload attachments or even send emails. In some cases mails were sent but an error popped up while sending an attachment. Google apps status page also updated to confirm that they received reports of issues related to Gmail and were resolving them. Users were inconvenienced for at least over an hour.
