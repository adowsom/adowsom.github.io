Shoaib Malik departs for England without meeting Sania Mirza & Son Izhaan in India वनइंडिया हिंदी

27 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

Shoaib Malik departs for T20 series without meeting wife Sania Mirza & Son in India. Shoaib shared a picture of the flight on his social media platform and asked the fans if anyone can guess where they are going. It is noteworthy that Shoaib was scheduled to go on a tour of England with his team in June but he did not accompany the team to India to meet Sania Mirza and his son Izhaan.
