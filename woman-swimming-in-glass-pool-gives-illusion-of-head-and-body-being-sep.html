Woman Swimming in Glass Pool Gives Illusion of Head and Body Being Separate Due to Refraction

30 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

This woman was enjoying a swim in a glass pool outdoors. Due to the refraction phenomenon, it looked as if her head, which was above the water surface and her the rest of her body underwater, were separate. She was giving the illusion of being headless until she came up to the edge to reveal it was an optical illusion.
