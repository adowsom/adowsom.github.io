Analysis of Rhea Chakraborty's exclusive interview

29 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

Rhea Chakraborty is the one person who everyone has been talking about since the death of Sushant Singh Rajput. The Central Bureau Of Investigation (CBI), the Enforcement Directorate (ED) and now the Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) are all investigating her. At a time like this, India Today’s Rajdeep Sardesai in a mega exclusive, spoke to Rhea Chakraborty on all the allegations levelled against her. Rhea categorically denied all charges, including the drug conspiracy and the siphoning off of Rs 15 crore, and more.
She made various claims during the interview which have raised various questions.
India Today TV analyses Rhea Chakraborty’s claims made during the exclusive interview. Watch for more.
