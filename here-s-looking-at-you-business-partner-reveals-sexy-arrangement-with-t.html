Here's Looking At You? Business Partner Reveals Sexy 'Arrangement' With The Falwells

30 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

Jerry Falwell Jr. is the son of evangelical leader Jerry Falwell, who founded Liberty University.
Falwell Jr. recently left his position at the university after posting a questionable photo on social media.
In it, he appears with pants unzipped, arm around a similarly unzipped young woman, and holding an alcoholic beverage.
Now, HuffPost reports a business partner of Falwell Jr. and his wife, Becki Falwell, has come forward with more scandalous allegations.
Giancarlo Granda says he had a years-long sexual relationship involving Falwell’s wife and the evangelical leader.
Granda says he was 20 when he met the Falwells while working as a pool attendant at the Fontainebleau Miami Beach hotel in March 2012.
Starting that month and continuing into 2018, Granda says the relationship involved him having sex with Becki Falwell while Jerry Falwell looked on.
In an interview with Reuters, HuffPost reports Granda produced evidence such as text messages and emails to support his claim about the long-running affair.
