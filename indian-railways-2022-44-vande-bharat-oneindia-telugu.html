Indian Railways : 2022 నాటికి పట్టాలపైకి 44 Vande Bharat‌ రైళ్లు! Oneindia Telugu

30 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

"Tender for manufacturing of 44 nos of semi high speed train sets (Vande Bharat) has been cancelled.Fresh tender will be floated within a week as per Revised Public Procurement (Preference to Make in India) order"ministry of railways tweeted.
