Woman Makes Disgusted Face Seeing Baby's Photo Before Realizing She Was on Video Call With Parent

26 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

This woman’s daughter pulled the "ugly baby prank" on her. She came up to her to show a friend’s baby’s photo on her phone. The woman looked at the photo and made a disgusted face. Seeing her straightforward reaction, her daughter told her that she was on a video call with the mother of the baby, and she might have seen her reaction. The woman got embarrassed and tried to cover her face, while her husband called the baby beautiful to cover up the awkward moment.
