Covid-19: New KL cluster linked to restaurant in Titiwangsa

27 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

The Health Ministry has detected a new Covid-19 cluster in Kuala Lumpur, which has three confirmed cases and is linked to a restaurant in Titiwangsa.
In a statement on Friday, Health director-general Tan Sri Dr Noor Hisham Abdullah said the first two cases were the restaurant’s foreign workers and the third was a 42-year-old Malaysian woman who is a customer of the restaurant.
Three other cases which were reported in Kedah were linked to the Tawar cluster, currently the biggest active Covid-19 cluster in the country.
