Parth Samthaan And Erica Fernandes To Quit Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2?

26 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

Ever since Kasautii Zindagii Kay 2 started airing on television, the show has kept audiences engrossed to their television screens; fans are in awe seeing Parth Samthaan and Erica Fernandes’ appealing chemistry as Anurag and Prerna. However, believing in the latest report, lead cast members Parth, Erica and Sahil Anand have decided to quit the show. Reportedly, Parth and Sahil have informed production about their decision but nothing has been confirmed yet, makers are trying their level best to convince them to stay back. Meanwhile, Erica who was working from home has resumed working from sets due to her parent’s health the actor might back out. Reportedly, the reason behind Parth’s decision is linked with his Bollywood debut, according to sources the actor wants to take a break to prepare for his big-screen project, "Parth has got some film apparently and he wants to prepare himself for that. He has not conveyed this reason to the production people but has discussed it with few of his co-stars on the set." Meanwhile, there were reports about Divyanka Tripathi taking over Erica’s character Prerna but the actor herself squashed the rumors through a social media post.
