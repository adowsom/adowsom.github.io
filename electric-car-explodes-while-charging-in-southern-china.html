Electric car explodes while charging in southern China

27 Aug 2020 by Eyygelena Fteras
Report

An electric car exploded while charging in southern China’s Sanming on August 20.
In the video, an orange car suddenly exploded when being sprayed with water to cool down.
According to reports, smoke came out of the car when it was charging.
After the firefighters received a report, they arrived on site to spray the water to the car to cool it down. But the vehicle suddenly exploded.
Fortunately, no one was injured. The case is in further investigation.
